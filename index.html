IT’S TIME FOR YOU TO SHINE!

Are you a great bookkeeper? Bookkeeping is the most underrated profession on the planet, and we want to change that with a competition that will recognize and reward Canada’s Top Bookkeepers.

SIGN UP

COMPETE

Great bookkeepers are the foundation for many successful businesses. We help owners and management sleep easy at night, knowing that their books and records are in good order. We monitor tax deadlines and cash positions and make sure vendors and customers are happy. Great bookkeepers often become controllers in growing businesses because keeping things “in Control” is the name of the game. Despite the important role good bookkeepers play, their value often doesn’t get properly recognized so we want to help change that. We also want to help great companies find great bookkeepers, another common problem.

If you know a great Bookkeeper get them involved in the “Search for Canada’s Top Bookkeeper”!

About the Competition

The Competition will be held online in the style of a “Hackathon” which is traditionally competitors for software developers or “Hackers”.

Competitor

There are two categories of Competitor: Professional and Amateur (Students). You can compete Individually or as a Team of Two.

Software

You can use any accounting software that you feel comfortable with.

Location

The Competition is completely online using HackHub.com- ie you compete from home.

Schedule

Each Region’s competition is held on a respective Saturday morning from 10:00am to 2pm. For practice events and training, Check Here.
